Our Company

PSR Group was established in 2000 and is one of the leading real estate companies in Bangalore. We have developed over 4 million sqft of residential, commercial and plotted development in the glorious 20+ years across prime locations in Bangalore.

The PSR Group adds to its credit a number of completed projects. To name a few there are PSR Arcade, PSR Pavilion, PSR Flora, PSR Garden, PSR Aster and many more in the list of 19 projects developed to date. Innovative construction and futuristic infrastructure is our forte.

With our passion for connecting people with property, we aspire to provide the ultimate real estate experience for today’s customers. We believe in earning the trust of our customers and building long-lasting relationship.

Our philosophy is very simple: we design projects around the needs of our customers.

All of our projects have been designed by Yukti Architects who are a highly experienced firm with a zest to deliver architectural services for all types of projects.
